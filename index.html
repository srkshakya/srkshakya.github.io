Who Am I?

Hi I'm Shrijan Rajkarnikar! I am currently a Project Control Officer at Ontario Teachers Pension Plan. At Ontario Teachers Pension Plan, I am helping to keep our technology current by leading the database migration and server decommission projects. Prior to Ontario Teachers Pension Plan, I was a Project Management Analyst at Investors Group, IT Project Control Coordinator at CIBC, and a Web Analyst at West Corporation. From these experiences, I have gained a variety of skills such as Project Management, Data Analytics, Software Development, Business Intelligence, and Quality Assurance.

I am also the managing partner for SRK Business Services, a company designed to help small businesses achieve technological and financial solutions. Lastly, I have recently graduated from the University of Toronto obtaining a Honors Bachelor of Science - Specializing in Machine Learning and Data Mining.
